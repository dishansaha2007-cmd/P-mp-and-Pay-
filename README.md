🎮 Pimp and Play 2.0 (PAP2)

Build your empire. Manage your agency. Dominate three iconic cities.

Pimp and Play 2.0 (PAP2) is a Python-based terminal simulation and business management game where every decision affects your reputation, finances, and influence.

Unlike traditional management games, PAP2 combines economic strategy, character progression, city-exclusive content, relationship systems, and movie production into one continuously expanding world.
